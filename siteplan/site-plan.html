Study247 Site Plan

Site Name

Study247 - This name was chosen because it represents an educational platform that helps students with study materials, schedules, and resources available 24/7.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Study247 is to provide students with a structured way to organize their studies, offering tools such as a timetable planner, study resources, and motivational content to help them succeed in their education.

Scenarios

  • How can I create and manage a study timetable online?
  • Where can I find useful study guides and resources for my courses?

Color Schema

Study247 uses a modern and professional color scheme:

  • #03045e (Dark Blue): Used for headers and navigation.
  • #0077b6 (Blue): Used for buttons and links.
  • #00b4d8 (Light Blue): Used for section backgrounds.
  • #90e0ef (Soft Blue): Used for highlights and cards.
  • #caf0f8 (Very Light Blue): Used for general background.

Typography

The fonts used on the website are:

  • Poppins (Sans-serif): Used for headings.
  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for body text.
